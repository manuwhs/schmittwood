How to get to Nürnberg (from Copenhagen)

Annoyingly there are no direct flights from Copenhagen to Nürnberg, so there are a few other options:


  1. Connecting flight to Nürnberg airport
  2. Flight to nearby airports
    • Prague: 4 hours train ride away
    • Munich: 2 hours train ride away
  3. Train: 10½ hours journey with change in Hamburg. Check connections on bahn.com
  4. Flixbus: 16 hours journey. Don't do it if you treasure your health
